What is EB-1A?

EB-1A is a first-preference employment-based immigrant visa category of the United States designed for individuals with Extraordinary Ability in fields such as science, technology, engineering, medicine, education, business, arts, and athletics.

It allows eligible individuals to obtain a U.S. Green Card without employer sponsorship or labor certification. Applicants must demonstrate sustained national or international acclaim and prove that their work benefits the United States.
